About Me!

Hello There! My name is Anika Khandekar. I'm a student of DPSI EDGE, and in MYP 2C. I love all things in technology basically coding and music. Now, moving on to why I created this website.
I created this website to inform you everything about cyberbullying.

My Mission

My goal is to raise awareness of online bullying. Why? I believe it to be significant since it has impacted so many people worldwide, and I feel that as the next generation, we should take action before it gets out of hand. In addition, we believe that everyone, regardless of whether they have experienced cyberbullying or not, ought to be aware of it in order to promote awareness and know what to do if they come across cyberbullying.
